\textbf{\Large Highlights}

\vspace{0.3em}
\textit{A duration-dependent flood depth-damage function calibrated using FEMA NFIP claims from three U.S. hurricanes}
\end{center}

\vspace{1em}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*,itemsep=0.5em]
    \item Richards damage function extended with duration-dependent parameters
    \item Calibrated on 295{,}637 FEMA NFIP claims from Sandy, Harvey, and Katrina
    \item Duration amplifies flood damage by 2.6$\times$ at 1\,m depth (36\,h vs 288\,h)
    \item MAE reduced $\sim$50\% vs JRC; Spearman $\rho = 0.949$ at ZIP-code level
    \item Framework applied to corporate credit risk via Archer Daniels Midland
\end{itemize}
